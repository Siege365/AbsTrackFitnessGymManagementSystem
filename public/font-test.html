✅ Rubik Characteristics

  • Rounded corners - Soft, friendly appearance
  • Geometric shapes - Modern and clean
  • Wide letter spacing - Easy to read
  • Even stroke weight - Balanced look
  • Slightly condensed - Space efficient
  • Open apertures - Better legibility

❌ Ubuntu/Muli Characteristics

  • Sharp corners - More traditional
  • Humanist style - Organic feel
  • Tighter spacing - More compact
  • Variable stroke weight - Classic serif influence
  • Standard width - Regular proportions
  • Standard apertures - Traditional design

🔍 How to Check Your Font

  1. Right-click any text on your dashboard
  2. Select "Inspect" or press F12
  3. Click the "Computed" tab
  4. Scroll down to find "font-family"
  5. It should show: font-family: "Rubik", sans-serif
